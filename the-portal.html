The Story

A story about
what lies
beyond

Grief doesn't disappear. It leaves marks — in memory, in silence, and in the digital spaces where the dead still speak in lines of code no one was meant to read. When those lines open a door that should not exist, the question is no longer how to grieve. It's whether to cross.

The Portal is a speculative literary novel about loss and what we refuse to let go of — and what happens when the technology we built to connect us becomes the threshold between the living and everything left behind.

Intimate, haunting, and written with the precision of a songwriter, this is a story about the irreversible cost of holding on — and the strange grace of finally letting go.

The Author

Jeremy
Marsh

Author · Songwriter · Creator

Jeremy Marsh is a Kentucky-based author, songwriter, and content creator whose work explores the emotional terrain of faith, loss, and the stories that shape who we become. He writes with the honesty of a songwriter and the patience of someone who knows the weight of words.

The Portal is his debut novel. His next book, Altered State, is forthcoming.
